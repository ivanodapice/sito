$$f_{x,y}=e^x(4x^2+y^2)$$

<p align="center">Calcoliamo le derivate prime :

   $$\cdot f_{x}=3x^{2}+3y^{2}-15$$
   $$=x^{2}+y^{2}-5$$

   $$\cdot f_{y}=6xy-12$$
   $$=xy-2$$
